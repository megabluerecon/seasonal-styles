Outfitter for All Seasons!

The colors of the seasonal images are as follows:

  • Spring: #2B7129
  • Summer: #EBA52B
  • Fall: #A81124
  • Winter: #005393

Using jQuery, create a page that promotes a unique look and feel for each of the four seasons by changing a page in the 4 following areas:

  1. A unique color scheme, for example stylesheet or background color, etc.
  2. A unique logo, for example a picture of a snowflake for winter, etc.
  3. A unique item of clothing, for example a parka for winter.
  4. A unique string of text identifying the season, for example "Leap into our Fall Wear!"
